Duane Newman

Coder - Speaker - Microsoft MVP - Scuba Instructor

Hi, I’m Duane. I build software, teach developers, support the tech community, and help people learn how to breathe underwater.

I’m a long-time software developer who loves learning and sharing the technologies that excite me. You’ll usually find me:

  • Speaking at conferences, user groups, and local events
  • Co-organizing the Kansas City .NET User Group
  • Providing hands-on technical training
  • Writing about software, tooling, and lessons learned on my blog
  • Sharing projects and experiments on GitHub

Microsoft has recognized my ongoing involvement in the developer community with the MVP Award, which I’ve been honored to receive every year since 2017.

I don’t just geek out over software.

In 2015, I fell in love with scuba diving, and today I teach others as a PADI & SDI certified Scuba Instructor through Dive Rinse Repeat Scuba. Helping people overcome fear and experience the underwater world for the first time is one of the most rewarding things I do.
